Reading Scores Range

Ranges of Scores by the students in the Reading Test

Scores between 70-79 dominated reading scores as proving that generally the 26.4% of all students managed to get within this threshold of score ranges. While this is a positive, the negatives are 90 students or 9% of the students failed the reading test and 91% scoring between 50 and 99

Math Score Ranges

Ranges of Scores by the students in the Math Test

Scores between 60-69 dominated math scores as 26.8% of students scored within this range. A pass rate of 86.5% is recorded in math as 865 students pass the test and 13.5% fail to pass the pass threshold of 50. This is the only subject which had students who failed to score more that 10 for the test
